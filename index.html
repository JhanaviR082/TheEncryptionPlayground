The Encryption Playground

This project highlights five of the most important algorithms in modern cryptography: DES, AES, RSA, SHA-256, and Diffie-Hellman. Together, they form the foundation of secure communication, data protection, and digital trust across the internet. From encryption to authentication to safe key exchange, these algorithms power the security of our digital world — and here’s an interactive, fun way to learn more about them!
